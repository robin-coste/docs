## What is Bastion?

Bastion is a comprehensive security and compliance platform designed to help organizations manage their entire security posture from a single dashboard. Whether you're pursuing SOC 2 certification, managing employee security awareness, or monitoring your attack surface, Bastion provides the tools you need.

## Why Choose Bastion?

- **Unified Platform**: All your security and compliance tools in one place
- **Automation First**: Automate evidence collection, policy distribution, and compliance monitoring
- **Real-time Visibility**: Dashboard views with actionable insights across all security domains
- **Integration Ready**: Connect with your existing tools (AD, Okta, Google Workspace, Jira, GitHub, and more)
- **Multi-tenant Support**: Partner mode for MSPs and consultants managing multiple customers
